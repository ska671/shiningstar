Montessori Centre

The Montessori section is for Children over 2 ½ years.

Montessori is system of nursery primary and secondary education devised by Dr.Maria Montessori in the 1950. She developed special teaching methods and equipments to maximize each child’s physical and Psychological potential at each stage of his/her development.

In practice this means that we divide our Montessori classroom into six areas Art, Practical life, Sensorial, language mathematics and culture (Which includes geography, nature and science) We give the children the freedom to choose from the different sections around the classroom, and we encourage them to complete their choosen activity. Working at their own pace giving children the freedom of choice, movement; speech and opinion within a well-structured environment means they are eager to learn, becoming more independent and self-confident.

Our Montessori qualified teachers work individually with each child, carefully charting his/her progress and recognizing when he/she is ready to more on to the next stage.
